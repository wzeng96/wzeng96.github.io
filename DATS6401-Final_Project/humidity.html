Humidity Plots

Analyses

From the first line plot, we can see that the highest value for humidity is 99.83 in Boston at year 2015, and the lowest value is 11.95 in Las Vegas at year 2017. Over the 6 years, the highest humidity values are always around 86 to 96. According to the weather descriptions, we can see that places with higher humidity values tend to have more rains and snows. The low humidity values are around 11 to 20, and Las Vegas has the lowest values for 2012 to 2017 due to its desert climate.

The second bar chart shows the average yearly information for each city over each month. We can see that the humidity values definitely decreases as we move to summer season, and the values start to increase when fall comes, and continue to increase until winter ends, which is around March or April. The highest range of humidity values occurs in June, with San Fransico has a value of 79.96, and Las Vegas has a value of 14.39.

By looking at the dot plot, we can see that overall, Vancuover has the highest humidity level among the 30 cities, and Las Vegas has the lowest humidity level. Most cities have humidity around 70, however, starting from Denver, the 6-years' average humidity drops to 50. And Albuquerque, Phoenix and Las Vegas can be considered as dry places.
