Conclusions

Based on previous information, places that have higher wind speed tend to have lower humidity values, places that have higher humidity tend to have higher pressure and lower temperature degree. Above shows 4 signicant cities' word clouds with their weather descriptions from 2012 to 2017. We can notice that even though the most frequent word is "sky clear", Miami and Vancuover also have "mist", "light rain", "fog" that indicate the level of humidity of the city. On the other hand, Las Vegas and Pheonix both show "sky clear" weather for more than 50%, with the rest of word showing almost equvalent frequency. These results are reasonable because Vancuover and Miami are near sea, the humidity level goes up, causing more rain. Since Las Vegas is located in desert, it's normal that Las Vegas has high degree during the day.
