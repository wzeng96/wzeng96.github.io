Wind Speed Plots

Analyses

It's interesting to notice the range of wind speed is between 0 and 7, this could be due to data-preprosessing. Both the highest and lowest wind speed occurs in 2014, with a value of 6.16 in Chicago, and a value of 0.414 Los Angeles, respectively. Los Angeles has the lowest wind speed for 5 years, and most places in the north part of the US have high wind speed. Looking at the bar chart, we notice cities all tend to have higher wind speed during winter time, and lower wind speed in the summer. During winter, the wind speed is around 4 for each month, and the wind speed is around 2 to 3 during summer. By looking at the third dot plot, we notice most cities have small differences, with a value of 1. Although the differences seems to be large in the plot, the overall range is from 0.606 to 4.23, therefore, the differences are small when we dive deep into it.
