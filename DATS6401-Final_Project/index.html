INTRODUCTION

Weather analysis and forecast are important because it not only helps people make future plans but also supports business for many companies, such as retailers and power industries. This project visualizes several weather features, including humidity, pressure, wind speed, and temperature for 27 cities located in the United States and 3 cities in Canada for over 6 years. Each tab shows the information of humidity, pressure, wind speed and temperature. Within the various aspects of analysis with this project, one interesting finding is despite the different locations of the cities, the pressure data seems to follow a normal distribution. Other analyses compare each feature over years, and make future weather predictions based on the information we obtained.
