Analyses

From the first dynamic line plot, it shows the average daily temperature values of each city by month for every year. The lowest temperature value is 5.29 Fahrenheit degree in February 2015 in Montreal. The highest degree is 93.39 Fahrenheit degree in July 2017 in Las Vegas. Over the 6 years, we can see that most of the lowest degrees are between December and February in Minneapolis and Montreal. Most of the highest degrees are during July and August. From the bar chart, the overall city that has the highest and lowest temperature degree is Miami and Montreal, respectively. During June and August, most cities have similar temperature, around 70 degrees. In winter, the differences between southern part and northern part of the North America is large, with a value around 50 degree. We can see in the third dot pot, Miami has an average degree over 6 years around 75 degrees. Minneapolis and Montreal has similar average degree of 42.5 degrees.
