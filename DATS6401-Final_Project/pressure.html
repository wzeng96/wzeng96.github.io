Pressure Plots

Analyses

From the line plot, we can see that most cities have pressure around 1000kPa, with the lowest value of 845.5 in Kansas City in 2016, and the highest value of 1062 in Vancuover in 2013. These do make sense because most the north america is plain area, and the pressure is around 1000kPa. By looking at the bar chart, it's interesting to notice that dryer places seems to have lower pressure as well. The high pressure places change often over month, however, places that have low pressure are Kansas City, Phoenix, Las Vegas, Denver, and Albuquerque, which are cities that have low humidity values. From the third dot plot, we can also see the cities' pressure are around 1000kPa, with Vancuover, Kansas City, Albuquerque and Denver have large range of pressure differences.
