About the Data

This analysis contains data from 10 countries in G-20, the timeline are from 2012 to 2018. The 10 countries are Australia, Canada, China, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, Russia, the United Kingdom and the United States. After doing data preprocessing, there isn't any missing values in this dataset. This project will analysis the spendings of each country in three directions, Education, Healthcare and Military. By doing so, we would like to see if there is any interesting trends or findings that are helpful to the whole world.
